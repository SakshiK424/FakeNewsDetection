Stay Safe from Fake News

Fake news spreads fast and can mislead people. Check the facts before believing or sharing information online.

Verify Sources

Check whether the news comes from a trusted website. For example, during the 2020 pandemic, false cures circulated widely on social media.

Check Dates

Old news can be reshared to mislead. For example, during elections, outdated incidents were circulated as current events.

Fact Check

Use fact-checking websites like Snopes or Alt News. False stories about celebrities or politicians often go viral without verification.

Think Before Sharing

Ask yourself: Is this believable? For instance, rumors about harmful products were widely shared on social media in 2022 causing panic.

Remember: Always verify information, stay critical, and help stop the spread of fake news.
