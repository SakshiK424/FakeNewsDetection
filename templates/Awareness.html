Fake News Awareness

1. Verify Sources

Always check the credibility of the source before believing or sharing news.

2. Cross-Check Information

Compare the news with other reliable outlets and fact-checking websites.

3. Watch for Sensationalism

Be cautious of exaggerated headlines, clickbait, or emotional language.

4. Check Dates and Context

Make sure the news is current and not misrepresented from past events.

5. Educate Others

Share awareness about fake news with friends and family to reduce misinformation.
